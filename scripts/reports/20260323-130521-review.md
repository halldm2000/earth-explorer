3. **State conflict between Tron + wireframe** — If wireframe is toggled on, then Tron mode activates, Tron saves the wireframe-modified colors (not the true originals). Disabling Tron then "restores" wireframe colors as if they were originals. Similarly, disabling wireframe after Tron overwrites would restore to pre-wireframe state, undoing Tron's green. This is a minor edge case but worth noting for future work.

4. **Only affects layers visible at toggle time** — Layers added after wireframe/tron activation won't receive wireframe styling. Acceptable for v1 but could confuse users who add GeoJSON after enabling wireframe.
